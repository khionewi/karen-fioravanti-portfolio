About Me

Hi, I'm Karen also known as Khionewi! I'm a web developer, artist, author and scientist. I love to learn new things and create beautiful works be it of art or a website.



Artist and Author, Khionewi Rivers, I creates worlds both visually and in writing that fit her cute and quirky personality. I am an artist and author that embraces the cute and quirky sides of me. Whether it be painting or writing, there is a reflection of me in all that I do.



I specialize in watercolors, oils, and pen/ink.



When I write it tends to be short stories or poems. I am influenced by H.P. Lovecraft and Eager Allen Poe.



I live with my family where the snow flies. We make our home with five bunnies.
